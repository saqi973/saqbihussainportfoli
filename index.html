About Me

Hi there! I am Saqib Hussain a Product Designer (UI / UX) with 2 years of experience across multiple industries. I am confident I can deliver exceptional results. My passion for creating user-centric designs has led me to identify product opportunities to increase financial accessibility and lead UX design on a company mobile app. I have also designed and implemented responsive layouts for web and mobile applications. In my previous roles, I communicated research findings, conceptual ideas, and detailed designs verbally and visually. I have designed wireframes and high-fidelity screens for an online platform that allows students to find and apply for jobs across the Middle East. I look forward to continuing to facilitate clients' product visions by researching, conceiving, sketching, prototyping, and user-testing experiences for digital products. I am committed to the success of each project and channel my energy proffering simple, human-centred solutions, and easy to implement aside from attaining set goals.
